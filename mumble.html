Current Status:

The Mumble wiki explains how to install and get past any issues you may encounter.

Once installed the 'connect' button in the window above should open the installed application and take you directly to the FLoD Mumble server.

If you cannot see the connected users window above then you may need to disable ad-blocker for this site (don't worry, there are no adverts!)

Running Mumble after installation but before pushing 'connect' is advised as a wizard may be launched to help with bandwith and microphone settings.

If any issues regarding passwords are concerned then please contact the admin.
